## Engineering Manager — Homework: Lightweight Case

## Preparation

We'd like you to prepare a brief case based on a product or system you have worked with recently, that gives us insight into your experience leading engineering or product teams and driving technical decisions.

## Case assignment

Please prepare a short artifact (choose one of the following formats):

- A one-pager (PDF or doc)
- A slide deck (max 5 slides)
- A short video (max 7 minutes)

The content should cover the following areas:

### Product/Project Overview

- What the product or system was designed to do
- The business or user problem it addressed

### Tech Stack & Architecture

- Key technologies and architecture decisions
- Any tradeoffs or constraints you had to navigate

### Key changes introduced

- Major new product development, key features, technical debt, or similar that was worked on during your time with the team

### Team Setup

- Size and structure of the team
- Your role and how you collaborated across functions

## What we're looking for

This should give us enough context for a technical conversation. We'll use it to ask follow-up questions about the technical decisions made, team delivery, decision-making, leadership approach, and how you balance technical and organizational concerns.

## Time commitment

This is meant to be lightweight. Please don't spend more than 1–2 hours preparing it. The goal is clarity and substance, not polish. We're looking forward to the discussion!

## Usage of AI

XYZ's vision is to become an AI-native platform where AI is embedded in our foundations. Hence, we highly encourage you to use AI when working on this case. Please be prepared to voice over and discuss which tools you used and how you used them. This will form part of the assessment and helps us understand your approach, reasoning, and how you leverage AI effectively.

Thank you!
